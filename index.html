About

Find Out More About Gapnet

Gapnet (Globalwatch application Network) is the online student recruitment platform of Globalwatch catalogue Ltd . The platform simplifies courses and universities search and applications process by connecting international students, recruitment partners, agents and academic institutions on one platform.

Established in 2012, we have experience team of staff to provide top tier services to our agents, educators partners and students.
